## 📊 ФИНАЛЬНАЯ БАЛАНСИРОВКА

### Economic Balance:

```
Income progression (per hour worked):
├── Junior: $5-10
├── Middle: $15-25
├── Senior: $30-50
├── Tech Lead: $40-60
└── CTO/Founder: $100+

Expenses progression:
├── Basic living: $2000/мес
├── Comfortable: $5000/мес
├── Luxury: $15000/мес
└── Ultra: $50000+/мес

Time to milestones (average):
├── First $1000: 1-2 hours
├── First $10K: 5-10 hours
├── First $100K: 20-40 hours
├── First $1M: 60-100 hours
└── First $1B: 200-300 hours
```

### Skill Balance:

```
Learning curve:
├── 1-30: Fast (tutorials, basics)
├── 31-60: Medium (practice)
├── 61-80: Slow (expertise)
└── 81-100: Very slow (mastery)

Time to max skill:
├── Focused learning: ~40 hours
├── While working: ~100 hours
└── Casual play: ~200 hours
```
